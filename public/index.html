Section 01

Information We Collect

LeaseGuard collects only the information necessary to provide our lease analysis service. We are committed to minimal data collection and transparent handling of everything you share with us.

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and a hashed password. We never store passwords in plain text.
  • Contract documents: Lease text or PDF content you upload or paste for analysis. See Section 3 for how this is handled.
  • Communications: If you contact us for support, we retain those communications to assist you.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and session duration — collected in aggregate and anonymized.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution for compatibility purposes.
  • IP address: Used for security purposes, rate limiting, and approximate geographic region (country-level only). Not stored permanently.

Section 03

Document & Contract Data

We understand that lease agreements contain sensitive personal and financial information. We treat your uploaded documents with the highest level of care.

  • Guest usage (no account): Contract text processed entirely in-session and never written to permanent storage. Once your browser tab closes, the data is gone.
  • Logged-in users: Your analyzed contracts are stored securely and associated only with your account. We encrypt all stored documents at rest using AES-256.
  • AI processing: Contract text is sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. Anthropic does not use your data to train models and processes it solely to return analysis results. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.
  • No human review: No LeaseGuard employee reads your uploaded contracts except where required by law or to investigate abuse reports.
  • No third-party sharing: Your contract content is never shared with, sold to, or accessible by landlords, real estate companies, data brokers, or any third party.
Section 04

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our service or as required by law.

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account, after which it is permanently erased within 30 days.
  • Saved contracts: Retained as long as your account is active. You may delete individual analyses at any time from your account dashboard.
  • Session-only data (guest): Not stored beyond the current browser session.
  • Anonymized analytics: Retained indefinitely in aggregate form with no personally identifiable information.
  • Legal hold: In certain circumstances we may be required to retain data longer if required by applicable law.
Section 05

Third-Party Services

LeaseGuard uses a limited number of trusted third-party services to operate. We have assessed each for data protection compliance.

  • Anthropic: Provides the AI language model that powers contract analysis and Q&A. Contract text is transmitted to their API for processing. Anthropic Privacy Policy ↗

We do not use advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or any behavioral tracking technology.
